# FAQ

## Is this a grocery delivery app?

No. Diana generates an editable weekly plan and shopping list. It does not place orders with retailers.

## Does Diana store my photos?

Photo handling depends on deployment configuration. See `docs/user/privacy.md`.

## Can I use it without AI features?

Yes. You can still manage inventory and required items. Plan generation and recipe replacement require the backend AI configuration.

## Can I delete my data?

Currently, there is no user-facing “delete account” flow in the app UI. Contact the maintainer (see `docs/support/support-workflow.md`) for data deletion requests.
